The "Tyre Change" Philosophy

Traditional training gives you a manual. We give you the tools and the vehicle. In this course, you are the one performing the task.

Theory

Reading the manual. Good for context, but rarely leads to mastery.

Observation

Watching a demonstration. Better, but you're still a spectator.

Experiential

Changing the tyre yourself. This is where the true learning happens.
